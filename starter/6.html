⚖️ Flexbox vs Grid (When to use?)
📐 Flexbox vs Grid guide:
FlexboxGrid
1D (row OR column)2D (rows AND columns)
Navbars, buttons, small componentsPage layouts, galleries, dashboards
Content-firstLayout-first
⚠️ Common Mistakes & Solutions
  • ❌ Forgetting display:flex or display:grid → no layout effect
  • ❌ Using absolute positioning for everything → breaks responsiveness
  • ❌ Not testing media queries on real devices → use DevTools
  • ❌ Missing viewport meta tag → <meta name="viewport"> essential for mobile
