⚠️ Common Beginner Mistakes
  • ❌ Missing semicolon → let x = 5 ❌ | ✅ let x = 5; ✅
  • ❌ Using = instead of === in condition: if(age = 18) → assignment, use if(age === 18)
  • ❌ Variable naming: let 1stName invalid → let firstName ✅
  • ❌ Forgetting to call function: greet vs greet()
  • ❌ Infinite loop: while(true){} → always update condition
